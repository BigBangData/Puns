How it works

You're given a question and must at least make an effort to imagine what a pun answer would be.

You press any key and an pun answer pops up. You must rate it on a growing appreciation scale from sigh to eyeroll to groan.
This rating populates the next question and the punishment continues.

Every ten puns you get unicorn confetti for your accomplishments.


Please visit the project's homebase in GitHub for a list of acknowledgements.
